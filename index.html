5 Signs Your Cat is Healthy & Happy

1. Good Appetite and Hydration

Your cat should eat regularly and drink water. Wet food helps with hydration.

2. Clean, Shiny Coat

Healthy cats groom themselves. Dull fur might indicate health issues.

3. Playful Behavior

Even adult cats should play daily. Lack of play can signal depression.

4. Regular Litter Box Habits

Consistent bathroom habits with no straining or accidents.

5. Bright, Clear Eyes

No discharge, squinting, or cloudiness.
